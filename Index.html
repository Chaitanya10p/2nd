About our School

Some four decades ago it was in the year 1977 that late Prof. M.H. Meer laid pious foundations of Christian Eminent School.
The institution aims at laying a sound educational foundation for an all round development of children, So as to prepare them for a brighter future. Their mental moral and physical development blazes a path for their dashing personalities in the coming future.
Christian Eminent School is a private recognized unaided English medium institution with primary, middle, high school and higher secondary.
